Hi i'm Guillaume Dejean

I'm a student in UI UX design with a background in ergonomics and mobility solutions currently living in Montpellier.

About

Me

The Past

For the past 13 years, i've been supplying mobility solutions and everyday technical aids for people with physical disabilities. This activity witch is very close to a Occupational therapist, allowed me to get a strong sense of understanding of users needs. I learned to listen and target constraints and to establish spécifications. Beside this job i've always been attracted by art, especially in design of any kind and music.

The Future

This year i redirect my career towards UX and UI design. These two disciplines combine parts of my background with finding to improve the user experience, be aware of the users needs, with the aesthetic reach in UI. During this formation i found my self a taste for coding that i didn't expect. In effect i like the rigor of the code and as well the magic that works when the code is revealed on a web page. My goal is, at the term of my formation to integrate a communication agency or a company where i can improve my skills and become a true UX UI professional.
